🔒 Introducing VERNEN™ — AI-Powered Legal Analysis for Pro Se Litigants

After years of navigating complex legal proceedings as a self-represented party, I built the tool I wished existed.

VERNEN™ is a multilingual legal analysis platform that provides:

▸ Autonomous document auditing against governing legal standards
▸ Standard of Creation (S.o.C.) methodology — every finding traced to statute, regulation, or professional code
▸ Field-level form guidance across 13 languages
▸ Six-pass analytical pipeline with finding classification (Critical / Major / Minor / Observation)

The platform applies professional skepticism to legal documents — the same rigor a qualified attorney would bring — and makes it accessible to people who can't afford representation.
